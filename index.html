Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This website serves to provide users with an in-depth understanding of the AI developed by OpenAI, named GPT-4. We provide detailed explanations and answers to frequently asked questions on this page.

Does Formli integrate with Quickbooks Online?

Yes. Formli is a drop in replacement for Quickbooks Payments. Using a tool like Zapier you can automatically reconcile payments made in Formli with the corresponding invoice in Quickbooks.

Can I pass the transaction fee onto the customer?

Yes. You can pass transaction fee specific to the payment method onto the customer.

For example, if you are charged a 3% fee for credit card transactions and a 1% fee (capped at $10) for ACH transactions, your customer will see a transaction fee specifc to the payment method they have choosen

Can I customize the fee per payment method?

Yes. This is a great way to incentvize your customers in choosing a low cost payment method.

For example, if you are charged a 3% fee for credit card transactions and a 1% fee (capped at $10$) for ACH transactions, you could choose to make the credit card fee 3% and ACH free.

Is it legal to add transaction fees onto a payment?

The law around credit card surcharges vary from state to state. Formli makes it easy to stay in compliance:

    You can offer a discount on payment methods like cash or ACH payments. This is legal in every state.
    You can choose to pass the actual transaction fee to the customer. This is legal in most every state
    Be sure to check with your state's Attorney General's office to see if there are any laws against surcharging.

What are the transaction fees for Formli?

Transaction fees for Formli range from 0.25% to 1.5% (plus any Stripe fees). You can check out the Pricing page for all off the details.

How do I get paid out for my payments?
